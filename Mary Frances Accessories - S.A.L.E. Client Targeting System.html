Client Buying Profiles

Analytical Buyers

Require detailed ROI analysis, case studies, and product trials before making purchase decisions.

  • Focus on detailed product specifications
  • Request performance data and testimonials
  • Prefer email marketing with technical details

Relationship Buyers

Value trust, referrals, and long-term partnerships over immediate transactions.

  • Respond well to personal outreach
  • Appreciate behind-the-scenes content
  • Value Mary Frances' artisan partnerships

Efficiency Buyers

Focus on time-saving solutions and streamlined purchasing processes.

  • Prefer quick online ordering
  • Value express shipping options
  • Appreciate bundled product offerings

Innovation Buyers

Seek cutting-edge designs and competitive advantages in their offerings.

  • Interested in new product launches
  • Value exclusive or limited editions
  • Appreciate trend forecasting content
