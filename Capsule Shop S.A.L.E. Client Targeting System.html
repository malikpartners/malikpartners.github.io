Analytical Buyers

Fashion-conscious professionals who research trends, compare prices, and read reviews before purchasing. They want detailed product information, size guides, and styling advice.

Approach: Provide detailed product descriptions, styling guides, and customer reviews.

Relationship Buyers

Loyal customers who value personal styling advice and trust your fashion expertise. They prefer personal consultations and exclusive access.

Approach: Build personal relationships through styling sessions and VIP experiences.

Efficiency Buyers

Busy professionals who want quick, convenient shopping experiences. They value curated collections and time-saving services.

Approach: Offer subscription boxes, quick checkout, and curated capsule collections.

Innovation Buyers

Trendsetters seeking the latest fashion innovations, sustainable materials, and unique pieces that make statements.

Approach: Showcase latest arrivals, eco-friendly options, and limited editions.
