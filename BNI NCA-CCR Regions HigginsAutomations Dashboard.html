BNI Regional Overview

California Capital Region (CCR)

BNI (Business Network International) California Capital Region's purpose is to help businesses, large and small, grow their business and make more money through a proven system built on structured referral marketing.

The region is built upon the philosophy of "Givers Gain®" - a unique culture of caring about giving business, professional development and building consistent, proactive and reciprocal business relationships.

Key Focus Areas:

• Gain new business through structured referrals
• Receive comprehensive business education
• Improve networking skills with positive business people
• Develop trusted business relationships and advisors
• Connect with members across multiple countries

2025 Achievement: Members reported income in excess of $10 million in new business from BNI referrals.

Northern California Region

BNI Northern California's purpose is to help businesses, large and small, grow their business and make more money! The region serves as the world's largest business networking and referral organization with a culture built upon the philosophy of Givers Gain®.

This region actively builds chapters across 18 Northern California counties, providing structured, positive, and professional referral marketing programs.

Counties Served:

• Butte, Colusa, Del Norte, Glenn, Humboldt
• Lake, Lassen, Mendocino, Modoc, Nevada
• Plumas, Shasta, Siskiyou, Sierra, Sutter
• Tehama, Trinity, and Yuba counties

2025 Achievement: Regional members reported $16 million in new business generated through BNI referrals.
