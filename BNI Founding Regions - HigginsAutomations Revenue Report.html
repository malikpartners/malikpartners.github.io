BNI Founding Region Overview

Business Network International (BNI) San Gabriel Valley and San Bernardino County represents the founding region of the world's largest business networking and referral organization. Built upon the foundational philosophy of Givers Gain®, this region has established a unique culture of caring that focuses on giving business, professional development, and building consistent, proactive, and reciprocal business relationships.

The BNI Founding Region's mission centers on helping businesses, both large and small, grow their enterprises and increase profitability through structured referral marketing programs. This systematic approach provides members with invaluable opportunities to gain new business, receive comprehensive business education, and improve their networking skills alongside positive, like-minded business professionals.

Members of the founding region benefit from speaking opportunities before audiences of motivated business professionals, allowing them to develop crucial presentation skills while showcasing their services. The organization facilitates the development of lasting business relationships and creates networks of trusted advisors who understand the value of mutual support and collaboration.

As part of the global BNI network, members connect and communicate with more than two hundred seventy-five thousand members worldwide, spanning across seventy-eight countries. This international reach provides unprecedented access to global business opportunities and cross-cultural networking experiences that extend far beyond local market boundaries.

The founding region actively focuses on building strong chapter networks throughout the San Gabriel Valley and Inland Empire areas. Members consistently report significant income generation through BNI referrals, with the region contributing substantially to the organization's global success metrics of millions of referrals passed and billions in business generated annually.

The region's commitment to excellence is reflected in its comprehensive support systems for both first-time visitors and established members. Through structured chapter meetings, educational programs, and networking events, the BNI Founding Region continues to set the standard for professional business networking and referral marketing excellence.
