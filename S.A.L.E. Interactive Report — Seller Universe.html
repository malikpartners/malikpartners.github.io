Origin Story & DNA

S Universe was built on helping product-based businesses—sellers—thrive, originally specializing in managing brands and scaling sales through Amazon and Shopify. NetSuite became their go-to ERP for back-end and financial management, which drove their expertise in the NetSuite space.


Over time, they expanded from retail into a comprehensive NetSuite solutions provider serving manufacturing, healthcare, non-profits, and general business. While retail remains core to their DNA, their transformation into a full-service NetSuite partner is the heart of what they do today.
