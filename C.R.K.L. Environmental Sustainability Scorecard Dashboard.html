C Collecting Data — Challenge Your Process

Comprehensive environmental data has been collected across 7 global manufacturing sites covering water, energy, GHG emissions, and waste recycling. The ESS Database provides quantitative metrics with weighted factors for normalized scoring.

Key Findings:

  • ✅ All 7 sites reporting environmental data with consistent metrics across 5 KPI categories
  • ⚠️ US and Canada sites report 0% renewable energy — critical gap identified
  • ⚠️ Asia Manufacturing shows lowest hazardous waste recycling at 63%
  • ✅ Water balance data complete with input, discharge, and loss tracking
  • ⚠️ Canada Manufacturing shows disproportionately high GHG intensity (35,865 tCO₂)
R Researching Gaps — Research Goals

Gap analysis against GRI Standards (GRI 303 Water, GRI 302 Energy, GRI 305 Emissions) and SASB Biotechnology & Pharmaceuticals standards reveals priority areas for improvement.

Priority Actions by Category:

  • 🔴 Renewable Energy (US/Canada): Deploy solar/wind installations at Platform 1 & 2 facilities. Target: 20% renewable by 2026
  • 🔴 GHG Reduction (Canada): Implement carbon capture, switch to natural gas co-gen, reduce intensity by 40%
  • 🟡 Haz Waste (Asia): Upgrade waste segregation systems, implement closed-loop solvent recovery. Target: 85%
  • 🟡 Water Intensity (Europe/PR): Install water recirculation systems to reduce intensity below 200 ML/M units
  • 🟢 Non-Haz Waste (US): Implement materials exchange program to increase recycling from 65% to 85%

🔍 Gap Assessment — Environmental Risk Mitigation

SASB Biotechnology & Pharmaceuticals standards gap analysis with GRI alignment

Critical Environmental Risks for Biomedical Products

🔴
Hazardous Waste Management (SASB HC-BP-150a.1)

Asia site at 63% recycling rate — below 80% industry benchmark. Risk of regulatory non-compliance and environmental contamination from pharmaceutical/biomedical waste.

Asia: 63% | Target: ≥85% | Gap: 22 pts

Mitigation: Install closed-loop solvent recovery, upgrade incineration systems, implement waste stream characterization per RCRA/EU Waste Framework Directive.

🔴
Zero Renewable Energy (GRI 302-1 / SASB HC-BP-130a.1)

US and Canada manufacturing sites operate at 0% renewable energy input. Significant exposure to carbon pricing risk and ESG reporting gaps.

US/Canada: 0% | Target: ≥20% | Gap: 20 pts

Mitigation: Procure renewable energy certificates (RECs), install on-site solar arrays, negotiate Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs) for wind/solar.

🔴
GHG Emissions Intensity — Canada (GRI 305-4)

Canada site at 35,865 tCO₂/M units — 2.7x network average. Major contributor to Scope 1 with 3,689 tons CO₂ direct emissions.

Canada: 35,865 | Target: ≤15,000 | Gap: 20,865 tCO₂

Mitigation: Transition to natural gas co-generation, electrify heating systems, implement process optimization for Platform 2 operations.

🟡
Water Stress & High Intensity (GRI 303-3 / 303-5)

Europe (212), Asia (191), and Puerto Rico (233) exceed water intensity benchmarks. Water-stressed regions require enhanced conservation.

Highest: PR 233 ML/M Units | Target: ≤150 | Gap: 83 pts

Mitigation: Install closed-loop cooling systems, implement water recycling for CIP/WFI systems, conduct water stress assessments per WRI Aqueduct.

🟡
Non-Hazardous Waste — US Site (GRI 306-4)

US site at 65% non-hazardous recycling — lowest in network. Packaging, single-use materials, and general waste streams need optimization.

US: 65% | Target: ≥85% | Gap: 20 pts

Mitigation: Implement source separation program, establish materials exchange partnerships, deploy waste-to-energy for non-recyclable streams.

🟡
Low EPI Scores — US & India (Composite Metric)

US (69) and India (66) EPI scores below 70 threshold. Multi-category underperformance requiring holistic improvement programs.

India: 66 | US: 69 | Target: ≥80 | Gap: 11-14 pts

Mitigation: Develop site-specific 3-year environmental improvement roadmaps with quarterly KPI checkpoints. Benchmark against Puerto Rico (97) best practices.

🟢
Environmental Disclosure Compliance (GRI 1 Foundation 2021)

Data collection infrastructure in place. All sites reporting quantitative metrics. Opportunity to enhance with qualitative stakeholder context per GRI 3 Material Topics.

Compliance: 85% | Target: 100% | Gap: 15 pts

Enhancement: Add narrative context to quantitative disclosures, publish GRI Content Index, align with SASB HC-BP sector metrics.
