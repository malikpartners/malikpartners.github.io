Assessing - Buying Pattern Analysis

Buying Profile Categories

Analytical Buyers (25% of prospects)

• Require detailed ROI analysis and case studies

• Need performance data and competitor comparisons

• Want trial periods or samples before commitment

Strategy: Provide technical specifications, cost-benefit analysis, and proof-of-concept materials

Relationship Buyers (35% of prospects)

• Value trust, referrals, and long-term partnerships

• Prefer face-to-face meetings and personal connections

• Make decisions based on vendor reliability

Strategy: Focus on testimonials, referrals, and building personal relationships

Efficiency Buyers (25% of prospects)

• Focus on time-saving and process improvement

• Want streamlined ordering and quick turnaround

• Value automation and workflow optimization

Strategy: Emphasize speed, convenience, and operational benefits

Innovation Buyers (15% of prospects)

• Seek cutting-edge solutions and competitive advantage

• Early adopters of new technology

• Willing to pay premium for advanced features

Strategy: Showcase latest technologies like Dimense 3D printing and holographic materials
